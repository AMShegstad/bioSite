Some good 'ol Q & A with Haley

Haley is my younger sister, and as the youngest of several siblings by more than a decade, she has a vastly different experience growing up than the rest of us did. I myself had a very different experience than did my two older siblings, but not due to a difference in age, but location. She and I share come marked similarities and at other times couldn't be more different. Having just started her college experience in 2022, Haley is a recent high school graduate who's world can sometimes seem unfamiliar to me. For this reason, I was very excited to ask some fun questions and hear her responses. Check them out for yourself below and get to know Haley!

Q.

How would you describe yourself in 5 words?

A.

"Nerdy, Hardworking, Independent, Neurotic, [and] Silly

Q.

Of which achievement are you most proud?

A.

"I would say that I am most proud of earning 'Husky Of The Year' when I graduated high school and made it out with $2000 and a brick to show for it"

Q.

Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

A.

"In five years, I picture myself living with my cat and my boyfriend (hopefully), working full time, and chipping away at college."

Q.

What do you want to do for a living?

A.

"I have no idea what I want to do for a living. I'm torn between a few fields that I find interesting like being involved in poltics, law, and genealogy"

Q.

What are your hobbies?

A.

"My biggest hobby would be gaming. I play a lot of online games on my computer, like Valorant and Fortnite. I also play card games like Pokemon and Magic: Ther Gathering. Otherwise, I sew and like to make clothes and I draw occasionally."

Q.

What isn't a hobby, but something you've always wanted to try?

A.

"I have always wanted to get into stained glass. It seems like such a unique medium that I would love to try... but I have no idea where I would start. I feel the same way about pottery, but I wouldn't know [where] to get clay or glaze, or where to find a kiln."

Q.

Best snacks?

A.

"If I'm in a snacking mood, I'll be eating cheese and salami. It's an iconic duo and if I'm feeling a little adventurous, I'll add some pickles or crackers"

Q.

Favorite movies, books, or games?

A.

" I already listed some games I like in [question] #5, but when it comes to music, I am obsessed with Sza. Last year, I was in her top 0.03% of listeners on Spotify. I'm not into many shows, I forget that TV exists, but I love [the] Avatar: The Last Airbender series and Breaking Bad. Any books or games that involve astrology I like because I'm really into the Zodiac and stuff like that."

Q.

What is something about you that you don't think enough people know, but they should?

A.

"Great Question. Honestly, I'm the type of person that if I want you to know something, I'll say it. I'm not cool and mysterious at all. I never stop talking."

Q.

What's the closest thing you have to a catch-phrase?

A.

"SLAY"
