Haley's go-to activity is gaming. She typically chooses titles such as "Fortnite", a free-to-play (ftp), battle-royale style offering whose victor is the player, or team of players, standing, and "Valorant", a popular first-person-shooter (fps) from Riot games, the creators of "League of Legends". She also enjoys playing card games like Pokemon and Magic: The Gathering. When she watches TV, she enjoys the likes of Breaking Bad and Avatar: The Last Airbender. When asked about activities that she is interested, but never tried, she did tell me that she has always wanted to get into stained glass, but wouldn't know how to start, as well as pottery, for which she listed the same barriers to entry.
